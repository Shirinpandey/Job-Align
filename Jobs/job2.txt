Job Title: Data Analyst

Company: HealthMetrics  
Location: Chicago, IL (Hybrid)  
Type: Full-time  

Description:  
HealthMetrics is looking for a Data Analyst to interpret healthcare datasets and generate actionable insights for our clinical teams. You’ll work closely with data scientists and IT to support data-driven decision making.

Requirements:

- Experience with Python or R a plus
- Bachelor’s degree in Statistics, Data Science, or related

Salary: $70,000–$85,000 DOE
